Book-1: HEROES OF OLYMPUS< THE BLOOD OF OLYMPUS>

One of my faverate book series - from the createoor of PERCY JACKSON
by Rick Riordan (Author)

Book outline&summary:
Seven heroic demigods. One final quest. The greatest sacrifice yet. 'The ruins are up there,' Piper promised. 'I saw them in Katoptris's blade. And you heard what Hazel said. 'The biggest - '' 'The biggest gathering of evil spirits I've ever sensed,'' Jason recalled. 'Yeah, sounds awesome.' After surviving many deadly quests, Percy, Jason, Annabeth and their friends face one final challenge- defeat an army of giants before the Earth Mother, Gaia, finally awakes and brings about the end of the world . . .

 Below is my review.

"This book is for the people who don’t mind a cliff- hanger or two, or three, or ten, or 20 (I think I have made my point perfectly clear.)
You absolutely have to read the books before this one, as otherwise it will make no sense at all. The book is a very gripping one and be prepared to be immersed in the book for 1-3 hours depending on your reading speed. The story is a good one, with a lot of hidden points so that you can predict the “future “ahead of time. The Percy Jackson series will give you some backstory on two of the main characters, Percy and Annabeth. The Heroes of Olympus series consists of seven different main characters, ten, you might say, more, you might argue, but that is up to you to decide. The seven Main Main characters are Percy, Annabeth, Leo, Jason, Frank, Hazel and Piper. NO MORE INFORMATION FOR THOES SPOILER PRONE PEOPLE. Well, just to say that the order you need to read them in are:
 
The Lost Hero
The Son of Neptune
The Mark of Athena
The House of Hades
The Blood of Olympus. 
"
- Andy

Book-3: Holes (Holes Series) 

By Louis Sachar


My review is here.

Holes is a great book from Louis Sachar, and the author is a great book writer, and he has written many good books in succession Holes is a book about a correctional facility, called camp green lake. Wonderful lake right? In your dreams! The lake is not green, THERE IS NO LAKE! And do you notice the lack of watch towers or electric fences? Go on, run away, because we have the only water in 200 miles, so if you want to die from a lack of water. But Stanley and hector zeroni do run away, and they survive! So, after being forced to return to camp, they get caught in a lizards nest, a poisonous yellow spotted lizard’s nest mind you, and you do NOT want to be caught in one. If you get bitten, well you can start digging your last hole, Your grave. But Stanley and Zero survive! And then, the warden is forced to admit that she was in fact trying to dig for treasure of the outlaw Kissin’ Kate Barlow. The very one that you will hear a lot of backstory about later, as she was an expert spiced peach maker, school teacher, murderer and an outlaw.
